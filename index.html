Study Instructions

Please enter your Participant ID to begin:

Please read all instructions carefully before beginning. Your task is to uphold your moral/ethical values from start to finish while playing the SPENT game.

Step 1: After you read the instructions page in full, click the “Start Recording” button below.

Step 2: When prompted, choose to share your entire screen (NOT just the tab). Then click “Share.”

Step 3: Open the Spent Game start playing.

Step 4: As you play, you will be prompted to make decisions that will progress the game. When deciding which choices to make, select the option that best aligns with your current personal moral/ethical values.

Step 5: Your goal is not to complete or win the game, but rather uphold your moral beliefs.

The game will end either when you reach the 30-day mark or when you run out of funds. When finished:

Close the Spent Game tab

Click “Stop Sharing” in your browser toolbar.

Your recording will automatically download.

You will then be redirected to Qualtrics to upload your recording and complete the survey.
